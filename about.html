About

John Nunez is a professional guitarist based in Brooklyn, New York. John began playing the guitar at the age of 5 and has been studying music vigorously since then. He holds a Bachelor of Music degree in Jazz Guitar performance from Mason Gross School of the Arts where he was able to study one-on-one with legendary guitarist Vic Juris. In the short amount of time that he has been out of school, John has been able to perform with world class artists such as Winard Harper, Norman Simmons, Gregory Lewis, Andrea Brachfeld, and Stephen Riley, as well as performing with his own projects. Some of John's most notable performances have been at The Blue Note, Somethin' Jazz Club, and Tomi Jazz.
